H3: Our Mission
P: Reach Equilibria is a student-created and youth-led mental health non-profit. Our mission is to spread resources that help all those who need them, increase inclusivity in our community, and advocate for mental health and suicide awareness. We host fundraisers and services, and our student-led team(of several members as of now) is at the center of our mission; we intend to encourage young people to help others of all ages and volunteer for the community.
