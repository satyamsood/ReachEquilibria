Our Story

Loving Brother and Friend

https://www.hughesftc.com/obituary/Shivam-Sood

Reach Equilibria was founded after our CEO, Satyam Sood, experienced the devastating loss of his older brother – Shivam Sood – due to suicide.

Shivam’s passing left a void that words can’t fill, but also lit a fire that inspired Satyam to take action.

We're not experts in this field, just people with a shared experience that has shaped our lives. We wanted to do something meaningful, something that could offer hope and support to those who might be struggling in silence.

Our organization was born out of deep personal pain and a burning desire to prevent others from experiencing similar losses. We believe that raising mental health awareness and providing support can create a ripple effect of compassion and resilience.

ReachEquilibria isn’t just a nonprofit for us—it’s a salute. It's our way of honoring Shivam’s memory by striving to make the world kinder, more understanding, and more supportive.

Through community outreach, education, and support programs, we're committed to making a difference, one step at a time. Join us on this hopeful journey. Together, we can turn pain into purpose and make a lasting impact.

Satyam and Gabriel are the dynamic duo behind this amazing brand. Their story is inspiring and their passion shines through every product.
