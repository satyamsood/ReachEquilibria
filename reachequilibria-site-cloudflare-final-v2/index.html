Who We Are

ReachEquilibria is a student-led Non-Profit organization that focuses on spreading mental health awareness and suicide awareness, with our primary target being teens and young adults. We host community events focused on mental health and connect organizations with larger non-profits that offer professional public speaking services. In the upcoming year, we are planning to host school speaking events featuring professional speakers, as well as other types of events.

Our Mission

Reach Equilibria is a student-created and youth-led mental health non-profit. Our mission is to spread resources that help all those who need them, increase inclusivity in our community, and advocate for mental health and suicide awareness. We host fundraisers and services, and our student-led team(of several members as of now) is at the center of our mission; we intend to encourage young people to help others of all ages and volunteer for the community.
